Credits

Greg Wilson has worked in industry and academia for 35 years, and is the author, co-author, or editor of several books, including Beautiful Code, The Architecture of Open Source Applications [Brown2011, Brown2012], JavaScript for Data Science [Gans2020], Teaching Tech Together [Wilson2019a], and Research Software Engineering with Python [Irving2021]. He was the co-founder and first Executive Director of Software Carpentry and received ACM SIGSOFT's Influential Educator Award in 2020.
